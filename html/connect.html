THE ADECCO GROUP

Plataforma Adecco Connect

Adecco Connect es una plataforma que centraliza los procesos de selección y la publicación de ofertas para recruiters de Adecco.

Introducción

Contexto y objetivos

My role was pivotal in bridging the gap between client requirements and effective design solutions. I played a key part in gathering specific needs from various countries using Adecco Connect. Through collaboration with development and business teams, I translated these requirements into actionable design solutions.

The goal was to enhance brand consistency and user trust by ensuring a unified and recognizable design language across platforms. Secondly, the system significantly sped up the development process, allowing for quicker responses to market demands and efficient feature rollouts. Thirdly, the system's scalability facilitated the company's digital expansion, ensuring adaptability to new platforms and technologies.

Retos

Adecco Connect was facing several issues: they were not using a design system, there were numerous usability issues and errors, the color red was being used incorrectly, and it was not responsive. To improve the product, I had to identify inconsistencies using Nielsen's heuristics and usability and accessibility rules to present the issues to various stakeholders.

Mi rol

As a Product Designer, I played a crucial role in bridging the gap between client requirements and effective design solutions, translating needs into actionable design solutions.

Proceso de diseño

Investigación y análisis

The process began with an exhaustive phase of research and analysis, where interviews with stakeholders and analysis of competitor design systems were conducted to understand their needs and pain points.

Definición de principios de diseño

Design principles focused on accessibility, reusability, and simplicity were established. These principles were crucial in the creation of all components, including the Time Picker, ensuring they were intuitive and accessible to all users.

Herramientas

Se usó Figma para colaboración y prototipado, Zeroheight para documentación de componentes y Storybook para documentar y compartir componentes con desarrollo.

Solución

The solution involved integrating a robust design system aligned with established usability and accessibility standards. Additionally, a redesigned user interface (UI) focused on clarity and engagement was introduced. This holistic approach ensured consistency, intuitive interactions, and inclusivity.

Test con usuarios y datos

La segmentación de participantes incluyó 16 sesiones de test de usabilidad con usuarios de Connect, analizando el flujo para crear un proceso de selección. Las funcionalidades analizadas incluyeron:

  • Configurar un nuevo proceso de selección
  • Programar una entrevista en directo
  • Añadir una prueba de hard skills al proceso

Hallazgos clave

The key points of the research included evaluating the overall experience of Connect and measuring the usability and satisfaction of recruiters with the new feature. The study aimed to uncover usability issues faced by real users, highlighting:

  • La tasa de clics erróneos en la funcionalidad de arrastrar y soltar vídeo fue del 50%, indicando que no se percibía como claramente visible.
  • Aunque los participantes valoraron la tarea como fácil, necesitaron 3 clics para lograr el objetivo.

Resultados e impacto

The solution, informed by user testing feedback, focused on better hierarchy in typography use, improved visual separation of sections, and more optimal use of spacing. Typography hierarchy is crucial for clear communication and readability, helping to organize information so readers can easily grasp the content structure and focus on key points.

Aprendizajes

The process highlighted the significance of interdisciplinary collaboration and user feedback in the development of design systems. Clear and accessible documentation is key to the system's adoption and maintenance.

Conclusión

In conclusion, the development and implementation of the TAG DS Design System in Adecco Connect marked a transformative journey, featuring challenges, learnings, and notably, significant strides towards achieving the vision of delivering exceptional user experiences. This project not only set a new standard in design and development within The Adecco Group but also reaffirmed the commitment to innovation, quality, and user satisfaction. With this solid foundation, there's enthusiastic anticipation for the future chapters in the digital transformation story.
