A bit about me

I am currently working at UST for The Adecco Group, an international company based in Zurich, with offices on all five continents and more than 10 brands.

My focus is on end-to-end solutions, from generative research to tracking post-release metrics and their respective iterations, with a special focus on interaction and behavior design. I enjoy participating in research and product management.

Having worked for various agencies, consultancies, and product companies, The Adecco Group has nurtured my professional and personal growth, with achievements such as launching a new multi-brand Design System covering the needs of five platforms, improving usability and accessibility of the platform I am responsible for by over 70%, and migrating the platform to the new TAG DS design system.

The design process for the DS was based on Agile methodologies and data-driven decisions, considering user, business, and development cost implications.

My current goal is to enhance my skills in accessibility and data visualization, aiming to bring valuable insights and inclusivity to our projects. Recently, I have achieved a certification in accessibility, underscoring my dedication to this area. My long-term focus is on improving internal design team processes to be more efficient, increase my seniority, and enhance my craft.

My hobbies

I love nature and dedicate all my free time to activities that involve being in touch with it. Surfing 🏄🏻 revitalizes me with the energy of the sea, while being in the mountains 🏔️ allows me to discover places that no one has ever set foot on before. I also enjoy traveling in my van. But without a doubt, one of the things I enjoy most is exploring and tasting the local flavors 🍕 of each destination. These experiences are not just hobbies, but a lifestyle that nourishes my physical and mental well-being, bringing balance and happiness to my life.
